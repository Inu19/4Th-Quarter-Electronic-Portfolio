📦 JavaScript Objects

Objects are containers for named values called properties or methods. They represent real-world entities with characteristics and behaviors.

var person = {
  firstName: "Grace",
  lastName: "Santos",
  age: 16,

Methods are actions that can be performed on objects. They are functions stored as object properties.

document.write()

📝 JavaScript Syntax

Syntax is the set of rules that defines how JavaScript programs are constructed.

  • Statements are separated by semicolons (;)

💃 Folk Dance

1. How does folk dance preserve Filipino culture?

Watching my fellow batchmates perform folk dances showed me how these performances serve as a living repository of Filipino heritage, passing down traditions, stories, and values from generation to generation. Each dance reflects the history, customs, and way of life of different regions in the Philippines. By observing these performances, I gained appreciation for how folk dance keeps our cultural identity alive and ensures that future generations understand and appreciate their roots.

2. What role does teamwork play in folk dance performances?

Observing my batchmates perform, I could see how teamwork is essential in folk dance as performers must move in synchronization, maintain proper spacing, and coordinate timing perfectly. Each dancer relied on others to create the complete visual and artistic effect. From the audience, I witnessed how success requires trust, communication, and mutual support among team members. Their collective effort demonstrated the importance of unity over individual performance.

3. What emotions are conveyed through traditional dances?

Watching my fellow batchmates perform traditional Filipino dances, I observed a spectrum of emotions being conveyed - joy and celebration in festive dances like Tinikling, grace and elegance in courtship dances like Pandanggo sa Ilaw, and pride in cultural identity throughout all performances. Some dances expressed playfulness and humor, while others demonstrated strength and resilience. The emotions reflected the Filipino spirit - warm, welcoming, and full of life.

4. How can we encourage the preservation of folk dances?

We can preserve folk dances by incorporating them into school curricula, organizing regular performances and competitions, and documenting them through video and written records. Encouraging youth participation through engaging presentations and making folk dance accessible and fun is crucial. Supporting cultural organizations, attending folk dance events, and sharing knowledge with younger generations helps maintain interest. Using social media and modern platforms to showcase traditional dances can also attract new audiences and participants.

5. What did you learn about the diversity of Filipino cultural arts?

I learned that the Philippines has incredibly rich and diverse cultural arts, with each region contributing unique dances, music, and traditions. This diversity reflects our archipelagic geography and varied historical influences. Despite differences, common themes of community, celebration, and resilience unite Filipino cultural expressions. The folk dance event demonstrated that our cultural heritage is not monolithic but a beautiful tapestry of different traditions that together form our national identity. It made me proud of our cultural wealth and motivated to learn more about different regional traditions.

🌏 Araling Panlipunan

📈 Lesson 1: Pambansang Kaunlaran

📋 Kahulugan

Isang estado ng bansa kung saan tinatamasa ng mga tao mula sa pinakamababang uri ng mamamayan ang nakakamanghang pagbabago sa ekonomiya at pamumuhay.

🔧 Mga Konsepto ng Pambansang Kaunlaran

🔄 Pagbabago

Paraan upang maging iba o naiiba. Proseso ng pag-iiba o pagkakaroon ng kaibahan sa lipunan at ekonomiya.

📈 Pag-unlad

Pagbabago mula sa mababa tungo sa mataas na antas ng pamumuhay. Pag-angat, pagsulong, o paglago sa pangkalahatang aspeto ng pamumuhay.

🎯 Pagsulong

Pag-usad tungo sa inyong layunin o goal. Pagpapaunlad ng kakayahan, kaalaman, at abilidad upang makasabay sa agos ng buhay.

🧬 Ebolusyon

Pagbabago sa mga katangian sa loob ng mahabang panahon. Anuman ang unang dumating ay mas mabuti sa sinundang panahon.

📊 Dalawang Konsepto ng Pag-unlad (Todaro & Smith, 2012)

🏛️ Tradisyunal

Pagtamo ng patuloy na pagtaas ng income per capita.

🚀 Makabago

Dapat na kumakatawan sa malawakang pagbabago sa buong sistemang panlipunan.

🏆 Mga Palatandaan ng Pag-unlad

  • Makatwiran at dinamikong kaayusan ng panlipunan
  • Kasaganaan at kasarinlan
  • Kalayaan sa kahirapan, hanapbuhay para sa lahat
  • Sapat na mga lingkurang panlipunan
  • Katarungang panlipunan

🌍 Human Development Index (HDI)

Pangkalahatang sukat ng kakayahan ng isang bansa na matugunan ang mahahalagang aspekto ng kaunlarang pantao. Kabilang dito ang kalusugan, edukasyon, at antas ng pamumuhay.

📊 Antas ng Kaunlaran ng Bansa

🏆 Maunlad na Bansa

Mga bansang may mataas na GDP, income per capita, at mataas na HDI

📈 Umuunlad na Bansa

Mga bansang may mga industriyang kasalukuyang pinauunlad ngunit wala pang mataas na antas ng industriyalisasyon

📉 Papaunlad na Bansa

Mga bansang kung ihahambing sa iba ay kulang sa industriyalisasyon
